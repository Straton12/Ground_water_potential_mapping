Limitations & Future Improvements

Data Limitations

  • Limited temporal depth in monitoring data
  • Incomplete technology classification (27%)
  • Spatial resolution constraints
  • Seasonal variations not captured

Methodological Constraints

  • AHP subjectivity in weight assignment
  • Simplified hydrogeological processes
  • Limited validation in remote ASAL
  • Static analysis without dynamics

Future Improvements

  • Machine learning for pattern recognition
  • Real-time IoT sensor networks
  • Higher resolution imagery
  • Climate change projections
  • Community participatory validation
